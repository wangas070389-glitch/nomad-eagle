# 🚀 Nomad Eagle | Mission Statement: Hero Precision Tuning (TTU)

## Mission Statement
**To** achieve high-speed user cognition (TTU) **for** potential household users **by** rebalancing the Hero Section hierarchy to ensure the product preview is visible above the fold and the headline reinforces, rather than dominates, the visual field.

## Core Functional Scope

*   **Information Hierarchy Refactor**:
    *   **Ratio Target**: 35% Headline | 20% Sub+CTA | 45% Product Preview.
    *   **Headline Scale**: Reduce font-size to 48–56px and weight to 600–700.
    *   **Headline Rhythm**: Force controlled line breaks for improved scannability.
*   **Vertical Spacing Economy**:
    *   Reduce vertical gaps/margins by ~30% to pull meaningful content (Dashboard) higher in the viewport.
*   **Visual Proof Elevation**:
    *   Incorporate the Dashboard preview directly into the Hero block.
    *   Use negative margins or 'pull-up' styling to ensure visibility above the fold.
*   **CTA Precision**:
    *   Integrate high-conversion microcopy: "Free • Takes 60 seconds."
    *   Increase contrast and scale for immediate accessibility.

## Anti-Objectives
*   **No Visual Drama over Utility**: Avoid massive typography that pushes proof below the fold.
*   **No Indiscriminate Spacing**: Stop using browser-default gap sizes that delay comprehension.
*   **No Uncontrolled Headlines**: Avoid headlines spanning the full width; limit to 800px max.
