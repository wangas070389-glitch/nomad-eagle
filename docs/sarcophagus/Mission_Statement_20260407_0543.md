# 🚀 Nomad Eagle | Mission Statement: Top 10% Conversion Optimization

## Mission Statement
**To** maximize conversion velocity **for** household users **by** grounding the marketing narrative in immediate visual proof, consequence-driven steps, and verifiable ledger trust.

## Core Functional Scope

*   **Consequential Narrative**:
    *   **Headline**: "See where your money is going—and where your life is heading."
    *   **Subtitle**: "Know if you're on track—and what to change before it's too late. Understand your future in minutes, not spreadsheets."
    *   **CTA (Primary)**: [ Start my plan ]
    *   **CTA (Secondary)**: [ See my future (60s) ]
*   **Visual Proof**:
    *   Integrate high-fidelity dashboard previews (using `dashboard_preview.png`).
    *   Frame the visualization as "Taking control of the trajectory."
*   **Outcome-Driven Flow (Add -> Know -> Fix)**:
    1. **Add your money**: Income, expenses, and goals in under 2 minutes.
    2. **See your future instantly**: Know if you're on track or falling behind.
    3. **Fix it with simple changes**: Adjust spending and see results immediately.
*   **Trust Reinforcement**:
    *   **Subtle Superpower**: "Numbers You Can Trust. Built on a financial ledger that keeps every transaction accurate and verifiable."

## Anti-Objectives
*   **No Passive Language**: Avoid "Track, plan, and improve" in favor of "Know, Change, Fix."
*   **No Abstract Benefits**: "Know what happens next" is out; "See your future before it happens" is in.
*   **No Generic SaaS CTAs**: "Start Free" is relegated in favor of "Start my plan".
