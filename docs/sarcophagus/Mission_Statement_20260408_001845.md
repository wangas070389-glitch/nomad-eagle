# Mission Statement: Household Financial Solvency Engine

## Mission
To transform speculative financial tracking into a **deterministic capital allocation engine** by replacing "Aspirational Math" with **Maximum-Utilization Solvency Models**. The objective is to ensure systemic liquidity through the rigorous enforcement of conservative balance projections that treat all Budget Limits as pre-authorized liabilities.

## First Principles
*   **Deterministic Solvency**: A state is only "viable" if it persists under worst-case spending scenarios (100% budget utilization).
*   **Conservative Baseline**: Unspent budget is a potential liability, not available liquidity.
*   **Utility Hierarchy**: Recognizing the asymmetry of consequence between Fixed (Hard) and Discretionary (Soft) capital.

## Functional Scope
*   **Mathematical Hardening**: Re-architect the projection engine to use the formula: `NetFlow = Inflow_min - (Outflow_fixed + ΣLimit_variable + Buffer_contingency)`.
*   **Worst-Case Trajectory**: Generate 12-month projections where the "Final State" represents a **Guaranteed Floor** of liquidity, rather than a probabilistic average.
*   **Variance Awareness**: Treat manual spending *under* a limit as a positive variance (Robustness gain) rather than a neutral event.

## Strategic Goal
To provide the Sovereign Household with a **Deterministic Safety Envelope**, giving them the statistical confidence to deploy excess capital without compromising survival-critical liquidity.
