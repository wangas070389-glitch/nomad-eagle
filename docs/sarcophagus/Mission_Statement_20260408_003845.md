# Mission Statement: High-Friction Deterministic Allocation Engine

## Mission
To engineer a **High-Friction Manual Allocation Engine** that transforms passive financial observation into active **Intentional Capital Deployment**. The objective is to eliminate "Passive Drift" by requiring a manual, logical handshake between every discrete outflow and a pre-authorized **Planned Entity**, enforcing real-time reconciliation of **Projected Wealth** against **Current Reality**.

## First Principles
*   **High-Friction Cognitive Awareness**: Manual entry is a feature, not a bug. It serves as a psychological gatekeeper, forcing the acknowledgment of capital erosion at the moment of execution.
*   **Atomic Entity Mapping**: The "Bucket" is the lowest level of resolution (e.g., *Netflix* instead of *Subscriptions*), preventing discretionary waste from masking as essential spend.
*   **Temporal Hardening**: Linking manual entries to "Planned Items" transforms **Speculative Projections** into **Hardened Facts**, ensuring the 2045 Wealth Projection is anchored in current deterministic variance.

## Functional Scope
*   **Relational Reconciliation**: Replace categorical tagging with explicit **UUID-to-PlannedItem** linkage in the database.
*   **The Triple View Architecture**: Implement a real-time monitor for every discrete entity:
    *   **Planned ($E_{limit}$)**: The maximum pre-authorized boundary.
    *   **Actual ($\sum T_{manual}$)**: Aggregated real-time consumption.
    *   **Remaining ($E_{limit} - \sum T_{manual}$)**: The Operational Runway.
*   **Recursive Determinism**: Instantaneous global recalculation of future wealth projection upon every manual entry.

## Strategic Goal
To transition from a "Digital Ledger" to a "Relational Financial Engine" that makes the long-term wealth impact of every single coffee purchase visually unavoidable and systemically deterministic.
