# 🚀 Nomad Eagle | Mission Statement: Value-First Landing Page

## Mission Statement
**To** provide immediate financial clarity and peace of mind **for** household users **by** delivering a simple, 3-step visualization of their money and their future.

## Core Functional Scope

*   **Mass-Market Narrative**:
    *   **Headline**: "See where your money is going—and where your life is heading."
    *   **Subtitle**: "Track, plan, and improve your finances in minutes."
    *   **CTA**: [ Start Free ] or [ See my future in 60 seconds ]
*   **3-Step "How it Works" Flow**:
    1. **Add your income & expenses** (Takes less than 2 minutes).
    2. **See your financial future** (Instantly understand your trajectory).
    3. **Make smarter decisions** (Adjust and improve your plan).
*   **Empathy-First Benefits**:
    *   "Your money, fully organized" (Clarity).
    *   "Know what happens next" (Confidence).
    *   "Private and secure" (Trust).

## Anti-Objectives
*   **No Technical Jargon**: Terms like "Immutable Ledger", "Provenance", or "Telemetry" MUST be translated into human benefits or moved to a secondary "How it Works" detail layer.
*   **No Empty Metrics**: Do not display $10M+ or 500+ households until these are real, verifiable project milestones.
*   **No High-Friction CTAs**: Avoid technical verbs like "Initialize" or "Simulate" in primary buttons.
